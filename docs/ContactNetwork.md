# Prague 1600: Assassin Guild

# Contact Network Design v1.0

## Purpose

The Contact Network is the primary long-term progression system.

Instead of leveling the player through traditional RPG skills, the player expands a network of allies, informants, specialists, and influential figures across Prague.

Core Fantasy:

The player becomes the unseen force shaping Prague through information, influence, and carefully planned actions.

---

# Design Principles

## Principle 1: Relationships Over Stats

Avoid:

- +5% Stealth
- +10 Damage
- +20 Health

Prefer:

- New mission opportunities
- New infiltration routes
- New recipes
- Better information
- Special events

---

## Principle 2: Every Contact Changes Gameplay

A contact should unlock:

- Knowledge
- Access
- Equipment
- Opportunities

Not numerical bonuses.

---

## Principle 3: The City Is The Progression

The player does not conquer Prague.

The player becomes connected to Prague.

The network itself is progression.

# Long-Term Vision

By late game, the player should feel that:

- They know the city.
- The city knows them.
- Every district contains allies.
- Every faction contains opportunities.

The network itself becomes the player's greatest weapon.
